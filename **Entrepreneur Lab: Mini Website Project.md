**Entrepreneur Lab:  Mini Website Project**

### **Assignment Instructions + Competency Summary + Student Checklist**

## **Assignment Overview**

For this project, you will design a strategic mini-website (3+ pages) for your venture. This assignment develops your skills in digital storytelling, brand communication, and clarity of message—core competencies for redemptive entrepreneurship in the digital age.

---

## **Project Requirements**

### **1. Choose Your Venture**

Select one venture to focus on.

Recommendation: Use the same venture you’ve been developing this semester, as your previous work will support this project.

### **2. Build a 3+ Page Website**

Use any free builder (Notion, Squarespace, Wix, Webflow, Canva etc.). Your site must include:

### **Page 1 — Homepage / Landing Page**

Must clearly communicate:

- Your brand story
- Your audience
- The problem they face
- Your solution
- A clear call to action
  Use the StoryBrand framework to structure your messaging.

### **Page 2 — “How It Works” Page**

Explain:

- Features and benefits of your offering
- A simple, visual step-by-step plan
- Expected outcomes or transformation

### **Page 3 — Strategic Page of Your Choice**

Choose a page that best supports your audience and business model. Examples: About, Pricing, Contact, Services, Testimonials, Portfolio, FAQs, Blog, etc.

### **3. Apply StoryBrand Brandscript Across the Site**

Messaging should be simple, customer-centered, and narrative-driven.

### **4. Record a Loom Walkthrough**

Record a 5–7 minute video walking through your site and explaining the intentional choices you made.

---

## **Competency-Based Assessment (Summary of Rubric)**

You will be evaluated in seven competency areas (100 points total):

1. Venture Definition & Focus (10 pts)

   Clarity of audience, problem, and solution.

2. Homepage Storytelling (20 pts)

   Strength of narrative, CTA, and StoryBrand application.

3. “How It Works” Page (20 pts)

   Clarity of features, benefits, and plan.

4. Strategic Third Page (15 pts)

   Relevance and alignment with your audience and business model.

5. StoryBrand Integration (15 pts)

   Consistency of messaging across the site.

6. Visual Design & User Experience (10 pts)

   Readability, cohesion, navigation, and overall polish.

7. Digital Presentation: Loom Video (10 pts)

   Clarity, intentionality, and completeness of walkthrough.
