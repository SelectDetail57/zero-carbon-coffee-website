# Zero Carbon Coffee: Complete Business Model Synthesis

This document provides a comprehensive overview of Zero Carbon Coffee's business model, target customers, value propositions, operational structure, and platform capabilities. It is written for someone with no prior knowledge of the company who needs to understand every aspect of how the business works.

---

## Executive Summary

Zero Carbon Coffee is a white-label coffee subscription platform that enables values-aligned organizations to generate recurring revenue by offering branded coffee subscriptions to their communities. The company handles the entire coffee supply chain—sourcing, roasting, packaging, shipping, and customer support—while partners simply receive revenue and deepen community engagement.

The core innovation is transforming an existing daily behavior (drinking coffee) into a sustainable funding mechanism for nonprofits, creative brands, and values-aligned businesses. Partners get a turnkey revenue stream with zero operational burden, while their supporters get premium, sustainably-sourced coffee delivered to their door.

## Part 1: The Business Model

### How Zero Carbon Coffee Creates Value

Zero Carbon Coffee operates a B2B2C (business-to-business-to-consumer) model:

1. **Zero Carbon Coffee (The Platform)**: Sources green coffee ethically, roasts it on electric roasters, packages it sustainably, and handles all logistics, payments, and customer support.

2. **Partner Organizations (The B2B Relationship)**: Nonprofits, churches, schools, content creators, and businesses who want to offer branded coffee subscriptions to their communities without managing inventory, fulfillment, or customer service.

3. **End Customers (The B2C Relationship)**: Supporters of partner organizations who subscribe to receive coffee. They interact with partner-branded websites but Zero Carbon Coffee fulfills their orders.

### Revenue Model

Zero Carbon Coffee uses a revenue-sharing model with partners:

- **Retail Margin Sharing**: Partners earn a percentage of the retail margin (the difference between wholesale cost and retail price). The default is 80% of the retail margin going to Zero Carbon Coffee and partners earning the remaining portion based on volume tiers.

- **Above-Retail Sharing**: If a partner prices above the base retail price, they keep 94% of that additional margin.

- **Zero Upfront Costs**: Partners pay nothing to start. Zero Carbon Coffee only earns when coffee sells.

- **Progressive Revenue Share**: As partners grow their subscriber base, their revenue share percentage can increase, rewarding community growth.

### The Value Chain

Every step of the coffee journey is managed by Zero Carbon Coffee:

| Stage | What Happens | Sustainability Element |
|-------|--------------|----------------------|
| **Sourcing** | Direct partnerships with farmers ensuring fair prices and ethical labor practices | Supports sustainable farming communities |
| **Green Coffee Storage** | Inventory tracking by origin, quantity, and quality characteristics | Traceability and quality control |
| **Roasting** | All roasting done on electric roasters | Eliminates carbon emissions from traditional gas roasting |
| **Packaging** | Compostable, recyclable, or post-consumer recycled materials | Zero-waste packaging approach |
| **Shipping** | Partners with shipping carriers for delivery | Carbon offsets for all shipping emissions |
| **Carbon Offsetting** | Calculates and offsets 200%+ of all carbon emissions | Every cup is climate-neutral |
| **Customer Support** | Handles all subscriber inquiries and issues | Relieves partners of support burden |

## Part 4: How the Platform Works

### Multi-Tenant Architecture

Zero Carbon Coffee is built as a multi-tenant SaaS platform:

- **Complete Data Isolation**: Each partner organization's data is segregated by organization ID
- **Custom Subdomains**: Every partner gets `{subdomain}.zerocarbon.coffee`
- **Custom Domains**: Partners can connect their own domains (like `coffee.yournonprofit.org`)
- **White-Labeling**: Dynamic theming based on organization brand settings (colors, logos, messaging)

### Partner Onboarding Flow

1. **Interest Form Submission**: Partner expresses interest, basic qualification scoring
2. **Organization Setup**: Create organization record, assign subdomain
3. **Brand Customization**: Upload logos, set brand colors, customize messaging
4. **Domain Configuration**: Optional custom domain with DNS verification
5. **Product Selection**: Choose which coffees to offer from the global catalog
6. **Pricing Configuration**: Set retail prices (can override defaults)
7. **Test Order**: Quality assurance before public launch
8. **Go-Live**: Automated readiness assessment and launch

### Customer Subscription Flow

1. **Browse Partner Site**: Visitor lands on partner-branded coffee site
2. **Select Coffee**: Choose from available products (roasts or categories)
3. **Create Account**: Email, password, basic info
4. **Configure Subscription**: Select frequency (weekly, monthly, bi-monthly)
5. **Enter Shipping Address**: Address validation via Google Address API
6. **Payment**: Stripe processes credit card securely
7. **Recurring Delivery**: Automatic renewals and fulfillment

### Order Fulfillment Pipeline

Once a subscription renews:

1. **Order Creation**: System converts subscription to fulfillable order
2. **Roast Assignment**: Items assigned to specific roasts from inventory
3. **Bagging**: Coffee roasted and packaged
4. **Label Generation**: Shipping label created via Shippo integration
5. **Packing**: Items packed in shipping container
6. **Shipping**: Handed to carrier for delivery
7. **Delivery Confirmation**: Tracking updates via carrier webhooks

Each step has operator tracking (who did it, when) for quality control.

### Payment Processing

Zero Carbon Coffee uses Stripe for all payment processing:

- **Payment Attempts**: Pre-created before Stripe API call for idempotency
- **Transactions**: Financial ledger records created after successful payment
- **Subscription Renewals**: Background job processes renewals on schedule
- **Refunds**: Direct refund capability through Stripe integration
- **Payment Method Storage**: Securely store customer cards for recurring billing

### User Roles and Permissions

The platform supports three user roles:

1. **SYSTEM**: Zero Carbon Coffee administrators who manage the entire platform
   - Manage all partner organizations
   - Access global roast and origin catalog
   - User administration across all roles
   - Domain verification and SSL management

2. **PARTNER**: Partner organization administrators
   - Manage their branded site settings
   - View revenue and subscriber metrics
   - Configure product offerings and pricing
   - Access customer information for their organization

3. **CUSTOMER**: End subscribers
   - View and manage their subscriptions
   - Update payment methods
   - View order history
   - Manage shipping addresses

## Part 5: The Product Catalog

### Coffee Origins

Zero Carbon Coffee tracks green coffee inventory with detailed origin information:

- **Country and Region**: Geographic source
- **Farmer/Grower**: Direct partner relationships
- **Elevation**: Affects flavor profile (higher = more complex)
- **Processing Method**: Washed, natural, honey, etc.
- **Varieties**: Coffee plant varieties (Bourbon, Typica, etc.)
- **Quality Score**: SCA cupping scores for specialty grading

### Roast Profiles

Each roast is a recipe combining green coffees:

- **Roast Level**: Light, Medium-Light, Medium, Medium-Dark, Dark
- **Tasting Notes**: Flavor descriptors (chocolate, citrus, berry, etc.)
- **Blend Composition**: Percentage of each origin in the blend
- **Decaf Option**: Available for applicable roasts

### Product Types

The platform supports multiple product types:

- **COFFEE_ROAST**: Single specific roast
- **COFFEE_CATEGORY**: Subscription to a category (e.g., "Light Roasts")—customer receives rotating selections
- **TEA**: Future product type
- **CHAI**: Future product type
- **MERCHANDISE**: Non-coffee items

### Partner Product Customization

Partners can customize products for their audience:

- **Custom Names**: "Morning Blend" instead of generic name
- **Custom Descriptions**: Tailored messaging for their community
- **Price Overrides**: Adjust wholesale and retail pricing
- **Activation Control**: Enable/disable specific products
- **Sort Order**: Control display order on their site

## Part 7: Sustainability Commitment

### The "Zero Carbon" Promise

Zero Carbon Coffee commits to being entirely carbon-neutral across the entire supply chain:

1. **Electric Roasting**: No gas emissions from roasting process
2. **Sustainable Packaging**: Compostable, recyclable, or recycled materials
3. **Carbon Calculation**: Measures emissions from farming, transportation, processing, and delivery
4. **200%+ Offsetting**: Offsets more than double calculated emissions

### Ethical Sourcing

- **Direct Farmer Partnerships**: Fair prices and ethical labor
- **Traceability**: Know exactly where coffee comes from
- **Quality Focus**: Specialty-grade coffees (SCA scoring)
- **Small Farmer Support**: Prioritize small-scale sustainable farms

## Part 8: Financial Model Deep Dive

### Revenue Streams

Zero Carbon Coffee earns revenue through:

1. **Product Margin**: Difference between cost and partner wholesale price
2. **Platform Services**: Handling fulfillment, support, technology

### Cost Structure

- **Green Coffee**: Primary COGS
- **Roasting Operations**: Labor, equipment, facility
- **Packaging Materials**: Bags, labels, shipping supplies
- **Shipping Costs**: Carrier fees (often passed to customer)
- **Platform Costs**: Technology infrastructure, payment processing fees
- **Carbon Offsets**: Environmental commitment cost

### Partner Economics Example

For a $20/month subscription:

| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Customer Pays | $20.00 |
| Base Cost (coffee, packaging) | ~$7.00 |
| Gross Margin | ~$13.00 |
| Zero Carbon Share (varies by tier) | ~$10.40 (80%) |
| Partner Share | ~$2.60 (20%) |

As partner volume grows, their share percentage increases through progressive revenue tiers.
