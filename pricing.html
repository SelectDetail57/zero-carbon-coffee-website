Frequently Asked Questions

What does it cost to get started?

Nothing. There are no setup fees, no monthly fees, and no minimum commitments. You only pay when coffee sells, and that payment comes from the revenue generated—not out of pocket.

How quickly can we launch?

Most partners launch within 1-2 weeks. Once you complete onboarding and brand customization, we can have your site live and ready for subscribers.

What if we want to offer custom pricing?

You can set your own retail prices above our base price. If you price above retail, you keep 94% of that additional margin—giving you flexibility to match your community's expectations.

How do we receive our revenue share?

Revenue shares are calculated monthly and paid out via direct deposit. You'll have access to a dashboard showing subscriber counts, orders, and earnings in real-time.
